# _KL Hokkien Mee - Black Noodles_

## Main Ingredients

*For 1 Packet Noodles*
- 10+ Prawns
- 200g pork neck - sliced
- 3 cloves garlic - chopped
- 2 cups chicken stock
- 3+ tablespoons black cramel sauce
- Soy to taste
- 1/4 green cabbage - rough chop, seperated 5x5cm
- Pork crispy lardons (pre prepared)
- 1 egg
 

### Step 1: Par cook prawns
    - Season salt + white pepper
    - remove when 1/2 cooked

### Step 2: Cook Pork + deglaze
    - Reheat pan + oil 
    - Cook pork , sear
    - Add garlic , cook till fragrant
    - Deglaze with chicken stock

### Step 3: Stew noodles
    - Preheat noodles in micro if from fridge  - 2mins
    - Add nnodles to pan with cabbage
    - Add cabbage
    - Add water if necessary
    - Add Caramel black sauce + soy to colour/taste
    - Adjust with sugar if necessary
    - Lower heat
    - Goal it to cook cabbage and have noodles absorb the liquid
    - Return prawns closer to the end of cooktime

### Step 4: Finishing
    - Add pork Lardon just before removing from heat
    - crack egg and stir in before serving, heat should cause egg form a silky sauce
