# _Ginger & Shallot Dipping Sauce_

## Main Ingredients

*In Ratios*
- 4x spring onion
- 3x ginger
  
- 5 x Neutral Oil (Rice Bran), enough to cover the aromatics
- 1 x Sesame Oil

- Salt to taste

### Step 1: Mince scallions & ginger

- Matchstick then finely mince
- Keep seperate as we will cook at different stages
 
### Step 2: Heat oil, cook ginger/spring onions

- Heat oil first
- Once shimmering add ginger
- 15s later add spring onion
- 20s+ just cooked, remove to a bowl

### Step 3: Season the sauce

- Add sesame oil
- Season with salt to taste
