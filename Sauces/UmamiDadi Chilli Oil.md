# _UmamiDadi Chilli Oil_

## Main Ingredients

*Yield: ~4 jars (180ml capacity)*

### The Confit Base & Aromatics
- 1 cup (250ml) Canola Oil (for the confit process)
- 2 shallots (120g), finely minced
- 1 head garlic (50g), finely minced

### The Infusing Aromatics & Whole Spices
- 1 cup (250ml) Canola Oil (for blooming spices)
- 1 piece cassia bark
- 2 star anise

### The Dry Spice Mix
- 1/2 cup Chili Flakes (Szechuan) (60g)
- 1/2 cup Chili Flakes (Korean Gochugaru) (100g)
- 2 tbsp Szechuan Peppercorns (3:1 Ratio Split: 1.5 tbsp Red, 0.5 tbsp Green)
- 2 tbsp sesame seeds (toasted separately)
- 2 tbsp Mushroom Granules

### The Texture & Seasoning
- 4 tbsp Roasted Soybeans (blitzed to a coarse, sand-like crunch)
- 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
- 1/2 tbsp Sugar
- 1/2 tbsp Sea Salt

---

### Step 1a: Infuse Aromatics (Confit)

- Mince shallot (120g) and garlic (50g) finely
- Cold start with 1 cup (250ml) canola oil in a small saucepan (small bubbles only)
- Cook for 30 minutes with constant stirring over medium-low heat
- Remove from heat and let sit for 1 hour to confit

### Step 1b: Infuse Aromatics (Whole Spices)

- Combine second 1 cup (250ml) canola oil with 1 piece cassia bark and 2 star anise in a separate saucepan
- Bring to a very gentle simmer over low heat and let bubble for 15–20 minutes until fragrant
- Remove from heat and discard the spent cassia bark and star anise

### Step 2: Prepare Dry Ingredients

- Toast red and green Szechuan peppercorns in a dry pan for 2–3 minutes; sift out black seeds and twigs, then coarsely crush
- Toast 2 tbsp of sesame seeds separately in a dry pan over medium-low heat until lightly golden and fragrant (about 2 minutes), moving constantly so they don't burn
- Mix together Szechuan chili flakes (60g), Korean gochugaru (100g), the crushed red/green peppercorns, the toasted sesame seeds, and mushroom granules in a large heat-proof bowl or pan
- Blitz roasted soybeans to a coarse, sand-like texture and set aside separately

### Step 3: Combine

- Strain the infused oil from Step 1a to separate the confit shallots and garlic from the liquid
- Combine the strained oil with the spice-infused oil from Step 1b in a saucepan
- Gently pre-heat the dry spice mixture bowl with the pan on low to warm the spices through before blooming
- Heat the combined oil over medium heat
- Pour hot oil directly over the warmed dry ingredient mixture to flash-bloom the spices

### Step 4: Season and Finish

- Add soy sauce, sugar, and sea salt to the warm oil mixture
- Stir to combine with the reserved confit garlic/shallots and the coarsely blitzed roasted soybeans
- Cool completely at room temperature
- Store in clean, airtight 180ml jars
- We want at least a 15% oil layer up top, top up with heated oil if necessary.
