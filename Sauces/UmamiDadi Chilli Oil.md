# _UmamiDadi Chilli Oil_

## Main Ingredients

*Yield: ~1.5 jars*

- 2 shallots
- 1 head garlic
- 4 tbsp Roasted Soybeans
- 1/2 cup Chili Flakes (Szechuan)
- 1/2 cup Chili Flakes (Korean Gochugaru)
- 2 tbsp Szechuan Peppercorns
- 2 tbsp Mushroom Granules
- 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
- 2 tsp Sugar
- 1 tsp Sea Salt

### Step 1: Infuse Aromatics

- Mince shallot and garlic finely
- Cold start with oil (small bubbles only)
- Cook for 30 minutes with constant stirring
- Remove from heat and let sit for 1 hour to confit

### Step 2: Prepare Dry Ingredients

- Mix together Szechuan chili flakes, Korean gochugaru, Szechuan peppercorns, and mushroom granules
- Blitz roasted soybeans to a coarse, sand-like texture

### Step 3: Combine

- Strain the infused oil
- Heat oil over medium heat
- Pour over dry ingredient mixture

### Step 4: Season and Finish

- Add soy sauce, sugar, and sea salt
- Stir to combine with roasted soybeans
- Cool completely
- Store in an airtight jar
